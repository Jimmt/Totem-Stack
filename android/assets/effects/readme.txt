"stars.png"
When a player lands a totem, stars of random colors will fly out in random directions in the following process.
1.  All the stars are randomly rotating
2.  Pop in effect (popin.png)
3.  Then do the pop in effect backwards (ie: slowly shrinks) when the stars disappear.
